# Privacy Sanitization

This repository is prepared as a public-safe version of the original project material.

## Removed Or Generalized

- Full personal names.
- Institutional front matter.
- Exact community, local-government, regional, and site names.
- Letters, approval pages, reports, CV pages, signatures, and interview screenshots.
- Map-based screenshots and location-specific visual layouts.
- Raw extracted PDF image dumps.
- Router and switch labels tied to real-world agencies.

## Kept

- Technical architecture.
- Device roles and selected platforms.
- Sanitized IP addressing examples.
- Sanitized router, switch, VPN, and camera setup references.
- Non-sensitive product/device visuals.
- Deployment, validation, security, and operations guidance.

## Public Asset Policy

Only files under `assets/device-images/` are intended to be public visual assets. Do not commit raw extracted PDF images, source screenshots, CVs, letters, maps, or scans unless they have been manually reviewed and redacted.
